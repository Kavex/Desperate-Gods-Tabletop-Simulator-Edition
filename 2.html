A) Getting Started
1.
Each player chooses a color and associated figure - Red Ranger, Blue Thief, Green
Knight, or Yellow Wizard.
2.
Give all the decks on the table a shuffle - hover your cursor over them and press the
shuffle key (‘R’ by default) a couple of times.
3.
Draw three cards from the Town Deck on the right side of the board and place them
face-up in the outlined spaces above the deck. These cards indicate which items are
currently available from the shop.
4.
Each player will roll a die to see who goes first - in the event of a tie, roll again.
B) Gameplay
1.
The first player rolls two dice, chooses ONE of them, then moves clockwise the number
of spaces their chosen die indicates.
2.
To get to the lower levels of the dungeon you can choose to follow the bridge from the
Kennel to the Guillotine and from the Labyrinth to the Altar. You may also move back up
from these areas in the same manner.
3.
The player then follows the instructions printed on the square they land on. If the square
requires the player to draw a card, the player draws from the deck which corresponds to
